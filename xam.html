MARIANA TRENCH SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL ADMISSION POLICIES

    General Policies

    1. The MTSHS maintains a student population of 40 students per class of each grade level.

    2. The only entry points to MTSHS Grade 11 via admission test.

    3. No one shall be admitted via Presidential discretion.

    Grade 11 Admission

    1. Grade 10 students who have completed their academic requirements are automatically admitted to Grade 11.

    2. Admission to Grade 11 for these applicants is based solely on the SHS Admission Test (SHSAT).

    3. SHSAT applicants must be currently enrolled in Grade 10 with an average of 85% or better (or its equivalent in another grading system) for three grading periods.

    Documentary Requirements for Application

    o Fully accomplished forms: application form, test permit and principal's certification (certification of average for the last three grading periods or 2nd trimester)

    o Two (2) recent 1.5" x 1.5" identical photos pasted in the designated boxes on the application form and test permit

    o Photocopy of the report card (Bring the original copy for verification.)

    o A long white letter envelope with postal stamp for mailing of results. Write the names of the applicant and his/her parent/guardian and address with zip code.

    Schedule

    o This advisory (which will include the schedule of activities regarding the MTSHS admission test, including the release of application forms) is usually posted (here and on our Facebook page) every January.

    Transfer Admission

    Former students who have attended another institution since attending the MTSHS must qualify on the same basis as new transfer students.

    Applicants seeking transfer admission into the institution have to satisfy the requirements of the institution as well as those of senior high school strands to which they seek to transfer. The following students are disqualified:

    1. students who have been permanently disqualified from the institution.

    2. students who have been dismissed for cause;

    3. students whose admission was withdrawn due to submission of fake/ falsified entrance credentials/ documents;

    4. students found guilty of dishonesty or any form of misconduct.



    New transfer applicants should submit the following requirements:

    • copy of transcript of records (TOR) or true copy of grades (TCG)from junior high school attended, for evaluation purposes (The TCG is to only satisfy initial application evaluation requirements but later on, a TOR shall be required to formalize and make valid the application, evaluation, and as the case maybe, the admission of the students).

    • accomplished MTSHS Form 3.1 (for foreign applicants) or MTSHS Form 3 (for local applicants). These forms are downloadable from http://www.our.mtshs.edu.ph/forms.html;

    • two passport size photos;

    • non-refundable fee of PhP100 for Filipino, PhP150 for resident foreign applicants or USD30 for non-resident applicants (all fees may be subject to change upon approval);



    Application deadline for foreign applicants: 2nd week of February

    Application deadline for local transferees: Last working day of April


    TRANSFER APPLICANTS ARE ACCEPTED IN THE FIRST SEMESTER ONLY

    If qualified, submit the following for the issuance of an Admission Slip:

    1) official transcript of records from all schools attended;

    2) TOEFL Official Score Report (if medium of instruction from previous school is not English);

    3) honorable dismissal (for local transferees only);

    4) study permit from the International Students Program (for foreign student);

    5) NSO Birth Certificate (for local transferees only
